* ###### 2022-01-11

  #### [Mother of slain student wins case against her roommate](2022-01/11/c_761843.htm)

  ##### The mother of a Chinese student killed in Japan in 2016 was awarded more than 696,000 yuan ($109,200) in a lawsuit against her daughter's former roommate on Monday after a court ruled the defendant played a role in the young woman's death.
* ###### 2021-12-30

  #### [Father, girlfriend get death sentence for killing children](2021-12/30/c_761876.htm)

  ##### A father who killed his two children by throwing them out of a high-rise apartment window in Chongqing last year, and his girlfriend who prompted him, were both given the death penalty on Tuesday.
* ###### 2021-12-10

  #### [Chinese court holds trial in absentia of embezzlement fugitive](2021-12/10/c_761915.htm)

  ##### A court in Central China's Henan province tried Red Notice fugitive Cheng Sanchang in absentia on an embezzlement charge Thursday.
* ###### 2021-11-24

  #### [Relics protection efforts deliver results](2021-11/24/c_761833.htm)

  ##### Tough punishment are handed out for offenses involving treasured items.
* ###### 2021-11-24

  #### [Subtitle website founder jailed for film piracy](2021-11/24/c_761914.htm)

  ##### The Shanghai No 3 Intermediate People's Court sentenced Liang Yongping, the founder of a website that hosted pirated films with Chinese subtitles, to three years and six months in prison on Monday for copyright infringement and also fined him 1.5 million yuan ($235,000).
* ###### 2021-09-13

  #### [Woman sentenced to death for series of killings](2021-09/13/c_761832.htm)

  ##### A woman who spent about 20 years on the run after being involved in multiple killings has been sentenced to death by a court in Jiangxi province.
* ###### 2021-08-31

  #### [26-year-old matricide seeks to appeal death sentence](2021-08/31/c_761831.htm)

  ##### Wu Xieyu, a former Peking University student who was sentenced to death for multiple offenses last week, expressed his dissatisfaction with the judgment and will appeal to a higher court, his lawyer said.
* ###### 2021-08-18

  #### [Court rejects claim against livestream host](2021-08/18/c_761841.htm)

  ##### A woman who became an internet celebrity while using her cousin's livestreaming account and then transferred the account to her own name has been cleared of infringing virtual property rights by a court in Guangdong province.
* ###### 2021-08-12

  #### [Court rejects appeal by Canadian drug smuggler](2021-08/12/c_761840.htm)

  ##### The high court in Northeast China's Liaoning province rejected an appeal by a Canadian drug smuggler on Tuesday and upheld his death sentence.
* ###### 2021-07-20

  #### [China court issues first ruling on personal bankruptcy](2021-07/20/c_761802.htm)

  ##### China's first ruling on personal bankruptcy cases took effect on Monday, about four months after the country's first local regulation on personal bankruptcy went into effect in March.
