* ###### 2020-12-29

  #### [Man who raped girl gets death sentence at retrial](2020-12/29/c_761821.htm)

  ##### A man who raped a 10-year-old girl was sentenced to death by the Guangxi High People's Court on Monday after a retrial, with the court withdrawing its previous ruling that he deserved a suspended death sentence.
* ###### 2020-12-21

  #### [Chinese court rules hydropower company to protect rare plant](2020-12/21/c_761829.htm)

  ##### A local court has ordered a hydropower company to strengthen the environmental impact assessment on a critically endangered plant before building a new power station in southwest China.
* ###### 2020-12-08

  #### [Hospital ordered to pay 760,000 to family in baby mix up case](2020-12/08/c_761826.htm)

  ##### A court in Central China's Henan province ordered a hospital to pay more than 760,000 yuan ($116,384) in compensation to a family of three.
* ###### 2020-12-07

  #### [Court orders return of stolen Zhang Gong Patriarch statue](2020-12/07/c_761824.htm)

  ##### A Chinese court on Friday ordered that the Dutch collector of the famous Zhang Gong Patriarch－a 1,000-year-old Buddha statue containing the mummified remains of a famous monk－must return the stolen relic to its original owner: two village committees in Fujian province.
* ###### 2020-12-04

  #### [Man who killed schoolboys executed](2020-12/04/c_761825.htm)

  ##### Huang Yichuan, the man who killed two 10-year-olds near the gate of an elementary school in Shanghai, was executed on Thursday, the Shanghai Intermediate People's Court announced.
* ###### 2020-12-03

  #### [Man sentenced to death for raping 4-year-old](2020-12/03/c_761816.htm)

  ##### A man surnamed Liu from Harbin, Heilongjiang province, was sentenced to death for rape by the Harbin Intermediate People's Court on Wednesday, according a statement released on the court's social media account.
* ###### 2020-11-26

  #### [Taiwan academic gets jail time for espionage](2020-11/26/c_761812.htm)

  ##### A mainland spokeswoman confirmed on Wednesday that Tony Shih, a Taiwan academic, was sentenced to four years in prison for espionage by a district court in Anhui province.
* ###### 2020-11-19

  #### [Retrial set for 3 who confessed to abuse that led to woman's death](2020-11/19/c_761819.htm)

  ##### The case of a woman who allegedly died due to frequent abuse by her husband and parents-in-law was sent back a court in East China's Shandong province for a retrial.
* ###### 2020-11-04

  #### [Ex-Party chief of Hohhot sentenced](2020-11/04/c_761827.htm)

  ##### Yun Guangzhong, a former senior official in the Inner Mongolia autonomous region, was sentenced to 14 years in prison for taking bribes on Tuesday and fined 5.5 million yuan ($822,220), a court in Liaoning province said.
* ###### 2020-11-04

  #### [Gang leader sentenced to death in Qinghai](2020-11/04/c_761828.htm)

  ##### A court in Qinghai province sentenced a leader of a mafia-style gang to death on Friday for more than 10 crimes, including intentional homicide, and handed 38 other gang members prison terms ranging from two years to life.
