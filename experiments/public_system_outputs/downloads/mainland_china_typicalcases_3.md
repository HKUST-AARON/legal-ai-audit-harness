  #### [Osteoarthritis treatment IP case solved in court](2024-12/06/c_1051210.htm)

* ###### 2024-05-16

  #### [Ningbo Maritime Court hailed for efficient resolution of maritime dispute](2024-05/16/c_990582.htm)

  ##### Ningbo Maritime Court, a specialized court for hearing first-instance maritime disputes in East China's Zhejiang province, has successfully handled a case of international maritime dispute, which involved the collision of two foreign vessels in the Strait of the Malacca.
* ###### 2024-02-22

  #### [Nation advances in protection of tech-related IP](2024-02/22/c_964478.htm)

  ##### More foreign enterprises have chosen Chinese courts to settle their patent and technology-related disputes, due to the equal protection offered to them in the handling of such cases, said an official with the Supreme People's Court (SPC).
* ###### 2022-07-26

  #### ['Luxury ban' prompts repayment a decade after ruling was made](2022-07/26/c_792208.htm)

  ##### After being refused permission to buy tickets for flights, a travel agency executive agreed to repay a debt of 200,000 yuan ($29,600) to an ex-girlfriend this month, about 10 years after he had been ordered to do so by a Beijing court.
* ###### 2022-07-20

  #### [Dutch collector ordered to return Buddha statue](2022-07/20/c_790991.htm)

  ##### A Dutch collector who says he bought the Zhang Gong Patriarch — an ancient Buddha statue containing the mummified remains of a famous monk — has been ordered to return the stolen relic to its original owners, two villages in Fujian province.
* ###### 2022-07-08

  #### [Man sentenced to death for murder of ex-girlfriend](2022-07/08/c_787276.htm)

  ##### A court in Yunnan province has sentenced a man to death for deceiving and instructing two people to murder his former girlfriend, it was announced on Thursday.
* ###### 2022-06-23

  #### [Man gets 18 months in prison for indecent acts against Alibaba employee](2022-06/23/c_772966.htm)

  ##### A man was sentenced to 18 months in prison by a court in Shandong province on Wednesday after he was convicted of committing indecent assaults against a female employee of China's e-commerce giant Alibaba.
* ###### 2022-06-14

  #### [Law equalizes compensation for rural, urban accident victims](2022-06/14/c_770579.htm)

  ##### Thanks to legal moves to unify standards for personal injury compensation, "Zhong Qing" (not her real name) was able to double her claim.
* ###### 2022-03-03

  #### [Firm owner jailed for illegal drug dumping](2022-03/03/c_761873.htm)

  ##### The owner of a pharmaceutical company in Shanxi province was sentenced to jail and fined for illegally dumping expired drugs.
* ###### 2022-01-11

  #### [Court orders divorced mother to live with, educate 8-year-old daughter](2022-01/11/c_761844.htm)

  ##### A court in Changsha, Hunan province, has ordered a divorced mother to live with her 8-year-old daughter, who had been forced to live alone with a nanny, marking the first case under a newly enacted law to promote family values.
