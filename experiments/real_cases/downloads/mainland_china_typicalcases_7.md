* ###### 2020-10-20

  #### [Court sentences 23 organized crime gang members to prison](2020-10/20/c_761817.htm)

  ##### The Changchun Intermediate People's Court, Northeast China's Jilin province, sentenced 23 organized crime gang members to prison terms ranging from two and half a years to 25 years for committing 12 types of crime on Monday.
* ###### 2020-09-03

  #### [Nine sentenced to jail over counterfeiting LEGO toys](2020-09/03/c_761871.htm)

  ##### A Chinese court on Sept 2 sentenced nine people to jail terms of up to six years over counterfeiting and manufacturing LEGO toys.
* ###### 2020-08-28

  #### [Former official gets six-year prison term](2020-08/28/c_761704.htm)

  ##### The first former civil servant China extradited from a European Union member state was sentenced to six years in prison for taking more than 52 million yuan ($7.5 million) in bribes.
* ###### 2020-07-29

  #### [Beijing court sentences counterfeit merchant](2020-07/29/c_761830.htm)

  ##### A court in Beijing sentenced a merchant to pay one million yuan (about $143,000) in damages to JD.com, a Chinese e-commerce giant, for selling counterfeit cosmetics online, the court said on July 27.
* ###### 2020-06-30

  #### [China's Hunan punishes defendants for mafia-style crimes](2020-06/30/c_761823.htm)

  ##### Seventeen defendants were given sentences, ranging from the death penalty to prison terms, for mafia-style crimes in central China's Hunan Province on Monday, according to a local court.
* ###### 2020-06-18

  #### [Real estate firm founder gets jail time for molesting child](2020-06/18/c_761810.htm)

  ##### A billionaire founder of a domestic real estate firm convicted of molesting a 9-year-old girl at a five-star hotel in Shanghai was sentenced to five years of imprisonment, the longest jail term for molestation under Chinese law, by a local court on June 17.
* ###### 2020-06-18

  #### [Former Hainan judge pleads guilty to bribery](2020-06/18/c_761809.htm)

  ##### Zhang Jiahui, former Hainan High People's Court vice-president, dubbed "the richest judge in China", pleaded guilty to taking bribes during a court hearing on June 17 in South China's Hainan province.
* ###### 2020-06-11

  #### [Special court instituted in China's Qinghai to hear environmental cases](2020-06/11/c_761822.htm)

  ##### Northwest China's Qinghai Province has instituted a special court that will hear environmental cases and provide legal services for the ecological protection of the Qilian Mountains.
* ###### 2020-06-05

  #### [Man sentenced to one year in jail for poaching rare fish](2020-06/05/c_761815.htm)

  ##### A court in Shanghai has sentenced a man to one year in jail for poaching and killing an endangered Chinese sturgeon, with a fine of 5,000 yuan (about 704 U.S. dollars).
* ###### 2020-06-05

  #### [Man who murdered wife, hid body, executed in Shanghai](2020-06/05/c_761814.htm)

  ##### A Shanghai man who killed his wife and hid her body in a freezer for three months was executed on Thursday, the Shanghai No 2 Intermediate People's Court said.
