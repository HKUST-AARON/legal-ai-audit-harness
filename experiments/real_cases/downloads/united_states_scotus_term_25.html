Opinions of the Court - 2025


The opinions collected here are those issued during October Term 2025 (October 5, 2025, through October 3, 2026). Opinions are posted on the website upon release in slip opinion format. Slip opinions remain posted until replaced with opinions edited to reflect the usual publication style of the United States Reports, including final pagination that will carry forward unchanged in the corresponding preliminary prints and the bound volumes of the United States Reports.
